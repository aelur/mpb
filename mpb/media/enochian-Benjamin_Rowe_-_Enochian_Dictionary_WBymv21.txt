1. Location/Source codes used in this list:

Numerical -- From the Enochian Calls, as listed in Geoffrey James' The
Enochian Evocation of Dr. John Dee. Numbers to the left of the decimal
represent the Call in which the word is found. Numbers to the right of the
decimal represent the word's position within the Call. All occurrences of a
word in the Calls are listed.
TF, TW, TA, TE -- the four Elemental Tablets
AE -- Liber Scientiae
T49 -- Tablet of the 49 Good Angels
HEPT -- Heptarchic Kings, Princes, and ministers of the hours
T12 -- The 3-by-4 table in the center of the Holy Table of Practice
SA - Sigil of Ameth
ZK -- Zodiacal Kings from Liber Scientiae
VV -- Crowley's Vision and the Voice
LAYCOCK -- Words listed in Donald Laycock's Complete Enochian Dictionary which
are not found in the above sources. Crowley's "phonetic" spellings of the
Enochian words were specifically excluded.
GT -- A few questionably Enochian words derived from Gerald Schueler's
"translation" of the letters around the Holy Table of Practice.
